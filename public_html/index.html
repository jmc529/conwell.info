ARIES Program

Pathfinder-Applied Researcher

The ARIES team works with virtual and augmented reality to create hands on learning experiences. I first came in contact with the team after my friend Dillon told me about a project he was working on with the team. The CI-SPY project used AR to recreate a, now mostly destroyed, historic black school. I think this sort of material is extremely valuable; any time invested towards creating new ways to learn and solidifying history in a way that may be more digestible is worth it. I currently am working on a few sports simulators where the project aim is to help injured or otherwise benched players refine their tactics.

WUVT

AM Director & Traffic Director 2014-2016

I began working at this radio station in 2013 in hopes of expanding my musical taste and deepening my musical knowledge. I later was offered the position of Traffic Director. As the Traffic Director I was in charge of a group of 16 individuals who, along with myself, were to create automated, yet tasteful, traffic (essentially playlists) for the station. As the AM Director I was in charge of 40+ individuals who were in the process of training to become certified DJs. During that time I created an online guide to assist DJs when I was not available. This guide is still in use today.
