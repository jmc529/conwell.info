Experience

622 North

Prep Cook 2018-Present

I began cooking here after hearing about, then trying, their delicious food. As I wanted to further improve my knife skills and palette it was a good fit. I've learned, and am mastering, different cuts (julienne, chiffonade, brunoise, etc), how to manage multiple projects at once, and, most importantly, stepwise cooking. Stepwise cooking is a term I use to describe executing one step before moving on. So if I need to make bruschetta, I will cut the onions tops and in half, then peel, then dice. This process makes the best use of my hands and time, ie; I am not picking up and putting down my knife needlessly.

WUVT

AM & Traffic Director 2014-2016

I began working at this radio station in 2013 in hopes of expanding my musical taste and deepening my musical knowledge. I later was offered the position of Traffic Director. As the Traffic Director I was in charge of a group of 16 individuals who, along with myself, were to create automated, yet tasteful, traffic (essentially playlists) for the station. As the AM Director I was in charge of 40+ individuals who were in the process of training to become certified DJs. During that time I created an online guide to assist DJs when I was not available. This guide is still in use today.
